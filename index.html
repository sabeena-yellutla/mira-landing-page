Who We Are

What is MIRA?

MIRA - Medical Intelligence Robotic Automation is a platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence, Robotic Process Automation, and modern Python-based web technologies to optimize healthcare operations.

MIRA automates key clinical and administrative workflows using RESTful APIs, data pipelines, database integrations, and web-based dashboards — with all processes executed robotically and automatically without any manual intervention.

This initiative enables healthcare professionals to focus on patient care while reducing manual administrative effort.

What We Do

Core Automation Features

MIRA automates key clinical and administrative workflows — eliminating manual effort across every touchpoint.

MyMedicare Registration

Automates patient registration workflows for MyMedicare, reducing administrative burden and errors.

Email Communication Automation

Handles automated email communications with patients and staff without any manual intervention.

Patient Billing Error Management

Detects and resolves billing errors automatically using data pipelines and intelligent validation.

Test Result Notifications

Automatically notifies patients and doctors of test results through integrated communication channels.

Post-Visit Feedback Collection

Collects and processes patient feedback after visits to improve healthcare service quality.

No-Show Management

Tracks and manages patient no-shows with automated follow-ups and appointment rescheduling.

Intelligent Patient Engagement

Uses AI to engage patients proactively, improving retention and overall healthcare outcomes.

Custom Automation Solutions

Develops tailored automation workflows designed to meet specific organizational needs and goals.
